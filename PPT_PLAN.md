# Presentation Points: Fake Internship Poster Detector

## Slide 1: Introduction
- Project Title: Fake Internship Poster Detector
- Need: Rise in cybercrime targeting students.

## Slide 2: Problem Identification
- High volume of fraudulent recruitment on WhatsApp/Telegram.
- Lack of awareness about common red flags.

## Slide 3: Proposed Solution
- A web application that acts as a first line of defense.
- Scans images for suspicious keywords and layouts.

## Slide 4: Key Features
- High-Speed OCR.
- Red Flag Highlighting.
- Multi-factor Confidence Scoring.

## Slide 5: Methodology
- Step 1: Image Processing.
- Step 2: Linguistic Analysis (NLP).
- Step 3: Heuristic Classification.

## Slide 6: Results
- Demonstrates >90% detection rate for standard "fee" scams.
- Fast processing (under 2 seconds).

## Slide 7: Conclusion
- Simple but effective tool for student security.
